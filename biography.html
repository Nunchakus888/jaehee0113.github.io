Brief

My name is Jae Hee Lee, currently working as a software engineer at Adelphi Digital ('AD').

Prior to working at AD, I worked as a software engineer and data science engineer for about 2 years.

I am a student at the Australian National University, pursuing a degree in law and information technology. In 2013, I obtained my bachelor of information technology. Therefore, I am an alumnus as well as a current student.

Apart from work, I enjoy learning new languages, composing music and watching dramas and movies.
